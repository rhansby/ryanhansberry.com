Creating a 3D Multiplayer Game in WebGL

Over the past month I and a few classmates have worked on our final project for our upper division 3D graphics course at UCLA. We chose to make a multiplayer game in WebGL, where players can compete against each other as tanks.

You (and friends) can play this game here, or check out the github page.

My classmates and I were all pretty proud of getting a relatively feature-complete project like this done in just over three weeks, so I wanted to expand a bit on the game's features and what I myself worked on.

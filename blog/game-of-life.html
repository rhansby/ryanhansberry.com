Conway's Game of Life

I recently wanted to make a small game, and also use Canvas, so I hacked this together in a few hours, using the awesome Easel.js.

You can learn more about Conway's Game of Life on Wikipedia.

How to Play

Click on cells to either "bring them to life" or "kill them" in order to create a seed. Alternatively, you can choose a default or random seed from the options on the left. When you're satisfied with the seed, click the Start button.
